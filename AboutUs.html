ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE:

People that been transformed by the Grace and Love of God,
filled with passion to come together and behold the Lord
in His Glorious Grace through worship, prayer, and fellowship.

We have been commissioned at the same time to reach and
disciple the unreached with the Gospel of Jesus Christ
and meet the needs of the local and global community.

WHAT WE DO

Prayer. Evangelism. Discipleship. Charity.

Worship/Prayer gatherings weekly.

Local and international outreaches.
